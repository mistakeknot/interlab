# Garden Metaphor Coordination Metric

**Primary metric:** `autonomy_score` (0.0 - 1.0, higher_is_better)
**Definition:** Ratio of autonomous decisions (no escalation) to total decision points in a task session

## Sub-metrics

1. **autonomy_score** — decisions made without escalation / total decision points
2. **coordination_conflicts** — file conflicts, redundant work, lost handoffs per session (lower_is_better)
3. **judgment_match** — blind evaluator agreement with agent's autonomous choices (higher_is_better)
4. **rework_rate** — proportion of work that needed revision after initial completion (lower_is_better)

## Measurement

Phase 1 is research-only (no quantitative metric). Phase 2 will define the benchmark script once the experimental protocol is designed.

## Baseline

Not yet established — Phase 1 research must complete first to determine whether the hypothesis is testable and what the right experimental controls are.
